## üé® VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### Circle Indicators
Use different colors for different types of actions:
- **Red** - Primary text input (names, titles)
- **Blue** - Date/time selections
- **Green** - Positive actions (success messages, agreement)
- **Orange/Yellow** - Settings/checkboxes
- **Purple** - Links and URLs
- **Cyan/Teal** - Optional enhancements

### Circle Animation Style
- **Initial state:** 0% opacity
- **Fade in:** 0.3s ease-in to 80% opacity
- **Pulse:** Scale from 1.0 to 1.1 and back (1s loop)
- **Border:** 4px solid color with 20% transparency
- **Glow:** 8px blur with matching color

### Arrow Indicators
- **Style:** Curved arrows with animation
- **Color:** Match the circle indicator color
- **Animation:** Draw from point A to point B over 0.5s
- **Use cases:** 
  - Pointing from form field to preview
  - Showing data flow
  - Connecting related elements

### Label Boxes
- **Background:** Semi-transparent white (90% opacity)
- **Border:** 2px solid matching the circle color
- **Font:** Bold, 14px, dark gray
- **Shadow:** Subtle drop shadow for depth
- **Animation:** Slide in from the side of the circle

### Checkmark Animations
- **Icon:** ‚úì in a circle
- **Color:** Bright green (#42b883)
- **Animation:** 
  - Circle draws from 0¬∞ to 360¬∞ (0.4s)
  - Checkmark draws with path animation (0.3s)
  - Slight bounce on complete (0.2s)

## üîä VOICEOVER RECORDING TIPS

### Tone & Pacing
- **Speed:** Moderate pace (140-160 words per minute)
- **Tone:** Friendly and professional, like a helpful colleague
- **Energy:** Upbeat but not overly excited
- **Clarity:** Enunciate technical terms clearly (PDPA, NRIC, verification)

### Key Emphasis Words
Emphasize these words to maintain engagement:
- "**Simple steps**" (Scene 1)
- "**Data privacy is crucial**" (Scene 3)
- "**Dramatically reduce errors**" (Scene 6)
- "**Automatically**" (Scene 8)
- "**Hours of manual work**" (Scene 8)

### Pauses
Add natural pauses:
- After each main step number (0.5s)
- After listing features (0.3s)
- Before the closing CTA (1s)

## üí° PRODUCTION NOTES

### Software Recommendations
1. **Google Vids** - Primary video creation tool
2. **After Effects** (optional) - For advanced circle animations
3. **Figma** - Design circle indicators and labels beforehand
4. **Audacity** - Clean audio recording and editing

### Screen Recording
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p minimum)
- **Frame Rate:** 60 fps for smooth animations
- **Cursor:** Use a highlighted/enlarged cursor for visibility
- **Browser Zoom:** Set to 90% to fit more content in frame

### Color Grading
- Increase saturation by 10% for vibrant look
- Ensure text remains highly readable
- Keep background slightly desaturated to emphasize form

### Background Music
- **Style:** Light, upbeat corporate background track
- **Volume:** Low (15-20% of voiceover volume)
- **BPM:** 120-130 for energetic feel
- **Avoid:** Music with vocals or prominent melodies
