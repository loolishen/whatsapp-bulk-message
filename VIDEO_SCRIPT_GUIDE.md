## üé• Production Tips

### For AI Talking Head:
1. **Use Professional Avatar Generator**:
   - D-ID, Synthesia, or HeyGen for realistic talking head
   - Choose professional business attire (button-down or blazer)
   - Neutral background or subtle branded backdrop
   - Maintain eye contact with camera throughout
   - Friendly, enthusiastic expression

2. **Voice Settings**:
   - Professional yet conversational tone
   - Moderate pacing (not too fast, not too slow)
   - Clear enunciation of technical terms
   - Slight pauses at transition points
   - Emphasize key numbers and benefits

3. **Position on Screen**:
   - Picture-in-picture bottom right corner
   - 20-25% of screen width
   - Circular or rounded rectangle frame
   - Subtle drop shadow for depth
   - Ensure not covering important UI elements

### Screen Recording:
1. **Software**: OBS Studio or Camtasia
2. **Settings**:
   - 1920x1080 resolution minimum (4K if possible)
   - 60fps for smooth scrolling and transitions
   - Cursor highlighting enabled (subtle circle or glow)
   - Optional: soft click sounds for button presses

3. **Mouse Movement**:
   - Slow, deliberate movements (not rushed)
   - Pause cursor on key elements for 2-3 seconds
   - Use zoom transitions for emphasis
   - No erratic or fast movements
   - Smooth scrolling (not jerky)

### Editing:
1. **Transitions**:
   - Smooth fades between major sections
   - Zoom transitions for emphasis on details
   - No jarring cuts or jumps
   - Consistent transition timing

2. **Annotations**:
   - Highlight boxes for key features (subtle border, no fill)
   - Animated arrows pointing to important elements
   - Text overlays for statistics (large, readable font)
   - Consistent color scheme matching brand
   - Numbers and percentages in bold

3. **Music**:
   - Subtle instrumental background music
   - Professional corporate/tech style (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)
   - Low volume (-20dB to -25dB, don't overpower voice)
   - Upbeat but not distracting
   - Fade in at start, fade out at end

4. **Pacing**:
   - Don't rush through features
   - Allow viewers to read text on screen
   - Pause on completed actions to show results
   - Use slow motion for impressive automated actions
